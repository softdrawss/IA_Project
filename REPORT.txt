Techniques implemented in this project:

- Flocking: Bees and Rusc
- Formation: Tourists following a Guide
- Ghost Follow: Sphere (not visible) that is followed by the Police Man that patrols
- Patrol: Police man patrolling and passing by 5 waypoints (and using GHost follow)
- Perception: Doctors wandering until they find the patient in the rocks
- Robbery: Robber trying to rob golden orb while police man is not near, police man wandering
- Wander: Villager wandering
